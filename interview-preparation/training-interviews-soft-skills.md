## Questions to Ask the Interviewer

### About the Role
- What does success look like in the first 90 days?
- What are the biggest technical challenges the team is facing right now?
- How is technical decision-making handled on the team?

### About the Team
- How does the team handle code reviews and knowledge sharing?
- What does the team's sprint/iteration process look like?
- How does the team approach technical debt?

### About the Company
- How does the engineering team collaborate with product and design?
- What opportunities are there for professional growth?
- What are the company's plans for the next 1-2 years?
